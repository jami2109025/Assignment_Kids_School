Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Developmental Focus: Early childhood education emphasizes social, emotional, and basic cognitive skills.Learning Activities: Play-based learning, introduction to letters, numbers, colors, and shapes.

Elementary School

Developmental Focus: Building foundational knowledge in literacy, numeracy, and basic science.Learning Activities: Structured lessons, group projects, and independent learning tasks

Middle (10-16) Years

Developmental Focus: Transition period with increased emphasis on critical thinking, specialization, and emotional maturity.
